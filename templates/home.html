Welcome to My Beekeeping Haven

Hello, and thank you for visiting! My name is Peter Hradil, and I’m a beekeeper living in the charming town of Lučenec, Slovakia. By profession, I’m a secondary technical school teacher in Podbrezová, traveling 100 kilometers weekly between work and home. But beyond the classroom, my true passion lies with the buzzing world of bees.

Beekeeping is more than a hobby for me—it’s a way of life. From tending to the hives in the quiet hours of the weekend to marveling at the delicate balance of nature, I’ve found joy, purpose, and connection through this ancient craft. Each hive is a universe unto itself, brimming with life, harmony, and lessons about resilience.

Through this website, I hope to share the wonders of beekeeping and the stories of my journey—the challenges, the triumphs, and the sweet rewards (including honey!). Whether you’re here to learn more about bees, explore the products of the hive, or simply discover a bit of inspiration, I’m glad you’ve found your way here.

Let’s celebrate the magic of bees together. Welcome to my beekeeping haven!
